You have been chosen to work on this intergalactic project, you should all be honored to be here! Since you will all be working in a spaceship, inspire yourself from the most sci-fi stuff you know of. We will try to keep a visual style similar to the famous Deep Rock Galactic corporation (more on that on the artstyle page)

Your mission is simple, some of you will need to work on 3D assets such as objects and structures you would see in a spaceship. 
Others will be asked to make our fellow crewmates move in certain ways to give them a charming personality.
Some will also need to crack the code and make logic for pretty much everything available in the ship. 

Don't worry, all of you will have better instructions on other pages, this is just the introduction!

We at SBF wish you the best success on this mission. Good luck!
