Since the game will be a cooperative experience up to 6 players (for the start, I only want 4), I want each character to be different, with some abilities unique to them.

**NAVIGATOR** 
Someone will be needed to conduct the ship, and the Navigator is the one for the job. He's the one in charge of pretty much every movement needed for the spaceship. 
Here is everything he has access to :
- Speed Lever (In charge of changing and adapting speed depending of the situation)
- 4 Emergency Brake buttons (In case something goes wrong! and to avoid activating it by accident, we put 4 buttons)
- Space Helm (No better way to move across the sea, also lets you go up and down)
- "Feather" Button (Gives you a boost upwards, just like when birds flap their wings!)
- Emergency Board (Let's you see if there is a part of the ship currently damaged. Also informs you of any bug in Navigator's system)
- Shield Board (Activating a shield is a must have when pirates and intergalactic threats are right in your face)

**ENGINEER**
Reactor damaged? A problem with the cannons? Or maybe the coffee machine is having a stroke? Simply ask the Engineer, he will be happy to help your repair anything (As long as food is involved)
When damaged, the Engineer can :
- Repair some of the Navigator's equipment (Helm, Feather button and Speed Lever)
- Repair the Reactors
- Refill Gas/Energy to keep moving or using Cannons
- Repair the small ships
- Can activate a sentry that can be upgraded

**CANNONEER**
- Has the ability to fly! 
- Has access to special cannons (Heavy weaponery)
- Can launch emergency missiles (On cooldown)
- Can help setup a shield (only active near the cannon bay)
- Has access to a small ship, equipped with offensive options (stronger cannon and mines)

**SEEKER**
- Can fly to move faster like Cannoneer
- Use a tracker to find nearby planets/enemies/loot (On cooldown)
- Also has access to a small ship that can recon, bring back loot, and do minor damage!
- Since Seeker is a quick one, he also got a better time to repair and reload.

**EVERYONE CAN...**
- Fire the cannons
- Use the ship's helm/Speed
- Reload cannons
- Reload energy
- Look at the Space Map
- Use the coffee machine
- Repair holes/damage(?)
